# Competitor Responses

## Kyrk Os

Kyrk Os focuses on tourism content and discovery; Tabylga connects discovery to verified payments and government-grade revenue analytics.

## Nomads

Nomads helps tourists find experiences; Tabylga builds the transaction and data layer that makes those experiences measurable and monetizable.

## QR-Payment

QR-Payment solves payment acceptance; Tabylga wraps payment into tourism routes, merchant onboarding, receipts, and public-sector dashboards.

## NomadSync

NomadSync can coordinate travel operations; Tabylga connects operations to live spending data across regions, categories, and merchants.

## Cashback Tourism

Cashback Tourism drives consumer incentives; Tabylga creates durable infrastructure for merchants, tourists, banks, and the Ministry.

## MobiGate

MobiGate is a mobile access or payment layer; Tabylga is a full tourism commerce network with analytics for regional development.

## Big Data teams

Big Data teams analyze what already exists; Tabylga generates clean tourism transaction data from the moment the tourist pays.

## AiTrek

AiTrek can personalize routes with AI; Tabylga closes the loop by turning routes into verified merchant revenue and policy-ready analytics.

## Baatyr Team

Baatyr Team may build a strong user product; Tabylga differentiates with the B2G dashboard, merchant network, and payment-backed data model.
